Receipt Printing

By default, receipts print through the browser — this can leave a blank gap on long receipts depending on your printer driver. Direct Thermal Print sends receipts straight to your printer instead, avoiding that entirely, but requires installing the free QZ Tray app once on this computer. The first time you print after installing it, QZ Tray will show a one-time "trust this site?" prompt — check "remember" and you won't see it again.

QZ Tray must be installed and running on this computer to detect printers.
